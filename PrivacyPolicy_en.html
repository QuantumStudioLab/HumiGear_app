1. Overview

The App is designed to discover, connect to, and communicate with nearby Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices.

  • No user account is required.
  • No personal information is collected, stored, or sold.
  • No advertising or analytics services are used.

2. Permissions and Data Usage

Bluetooth / Nearby Devices

The App uses Bluetooth permissions to scan for, connect to, and exchange data with BLE devices selected by the user.

Location Permission (Platform-specific)

On some platforms (such as certain versions of Android), the operating system requires location permission to perform Bluetooth device scanning. This permission is used solely to enable discovery of nearby Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices.
On iOS, the App does not request or use location permission for Bluetooth functionality.

The App does not collect, store,track, or share any location data. Location permission is used solely to enable Bluetooth device discovery.

Data from BLE Devices

The App may read and display data transmitted by connected BLE devices (such as sensor values or configuration parameters). This data is processed locally on the user's device and is not transmitted to our servers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties. The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting SDKs.

4. Data Retention

The App does not retain personal data. Any device data is handled temporarily in memory for display or configuration purposes only.

5. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect the App. However, no software system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not specifically designed for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
